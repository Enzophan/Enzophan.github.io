Unwavering Consistency and Reliability

Automated tests eliminate the risk of human error inherent in manual checking. They perform the exact same steps in the exact same way, every single time, providing a trustworthy and objective quality gate for every build.

Manual Testing Risks

  • Susceptible to human error and missed steps.
  • Execution can vary between different testers.
  • Results can be subjective.
  • Difficult to perform consistently under pressure.

Automation Guarantees

  • Executes tests precisely as programmed, every time.
  • Provides 100% repeatable and consistent results.
  • Offers objective, data-driven pass/fail outcomes.
  • Acts as a reliable, automated quality gate.
